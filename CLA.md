## Why this CLA exists

PocketDev is currently licensed under the Functional Source License (FSL-1.1-Apache-2.0).
The FSL is source-available rather than fully open source — its single restriction is that
you cannot publish the app to any app store (Apple App Store, Google Play, etc.).

This restriction exists as a launch-time precaution against app store pirates: someone
quickly re-skinning PocketDev and publishing it before the project has a community large
enough to defend itself. It is not a permanent stance.

The plan is to relicense PocketDev to a fully open source license (Apache 2.0 or MIT) once
a community has formed behind the project — at that point, the community itself becomes the
best defense against bad-faith forks. The FSL already converts to Apache 2.0 automatically
after two years per version, but relicensing may happen sooner if community momentum
warrants it.

This CLA gives the Licensor the right to make that transition without needing to track down
every contributor for permission. By signing, you are actively enabling that future.
